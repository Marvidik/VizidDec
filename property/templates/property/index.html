Our Working Process

Consultation: Discuss project needs, preferences, and budget with the client.
Design Proposal: Create a tailored design proposal based on client requirements.
Approval: Client reviews and approves the design proposal.
Project Management: Assign a dedicated manager to oversee the process.
Procurement: Source materials and provide client with samples.
Installation: Coordinate and execute the project's installation.
Final Approval: Conduct a walkthrough with the client for project approval.
Follow-Up: Ensure client satisfaction and provide ongoing support.
